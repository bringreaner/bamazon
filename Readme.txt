This app displays a table of items similar to Amazon.
The customer selects the id of the item they wish to purchase.
The app checks the database to see if there is enough quantity for the item for the customer to purchase.
If there is not, the customer is alerted that there is insufficient quantity for them to purchase.
If there is enough, the customer is alerted of the total price of their request, and the database is updated to reflect the amount the customer purchased.
